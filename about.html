About Us

Crowntrack is a fleet management solutions provider based in Kenya. We have a passionate and skilled team of professionals who have excelled in the field of asset management and monitoring, logistics and mobile fleet management.

We specialise in the emerging markets within the entire supply chain to bring you value and reliability with our wide range of products tailor-made to fit the exact customer needs.

Our solutions are based on the latest technologies in the market. We are pioneers of new products in the East African Market. We have proven experience in deployment of the following technologies: IoT Connectivity, CANBus Solutions, BLE Sensors technology, OTA Hardware among other new technologies.
